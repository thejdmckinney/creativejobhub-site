## üéØ **TITLE TAG BEST PRACTICES**

### **Elements to Include (Pick 3-4):**
1. **Industry keyword** (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical)
2. **Price comparison** ($29/mo vs Competitor $X)
3. **Top benefit** (Save $X/year, Book X% more jobs)
4. **CTA** (Free Trial, Try Free, Start Free)
5. **Competitor name** (vs Jobber, vs ServiceTitan)

### **Length Guidelines:**
- **Ideal:** 50-60 characters (shows fully on desktop + mobile)
- **Maximum:** 70 characters (may truncate on mobile)
- **Your homepage:** 78 chars (slightly long but worth it for the message)

### **What Gets Clicks:**
- **Numbers:** "Save $3,600" > "Save money"
- **Specifics:** "$29/mo" > "affordable"
- **Comparisons:** "vs Jobber $329" > just "$29"
- **Brackets:** "[Free Trial]" > "Free Trial"
