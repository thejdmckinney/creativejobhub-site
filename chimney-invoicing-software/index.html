Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up recurring annual chimney invoices?

Set up annual recurring invoices for each customer once, and the system automatically generates and emails their invoice after each chimney service. You can customize the service description (inspection, cleaning, repair), include CSIA inspection reports as PDF attachments, and add photos of creosote buildup or damage. The invoice includes all service details, creosote levels found, and any recommended repairs.

Do I need a merchant account for online chimney payments?

No, online payment processing is built-in to Creative Job Hub. Customers can pay chimney invoices with credit card, debit card, or ACH bank transfer at the industry-standard rate of 2.9% + 30¬¢ per transaction. You get paid in 3 days on average versus 30-45 days with mailed checks. No separate merchant account, payment gateway, or integration needed.

How do CSIA inspection reports attach to invoices?

When your technician completes a CSIA Level 1, 2, or 3 inspection checklist on-site, the system automatically generates a professional PDF report with inspection findings, creosote levels, photos, and CSIA compliance details. This report attaches to the invoice when emailed to the customer, providing complete documentation for insurance claims and compliance records.

Does chimney invoicing sync with QuickBooks?

Yes, two-way QuickBooks sync keeps your chimney business accounting in perfect sync. Invoices, payments, and customer information sync automatically in real-time. When a customer pays a chimney invoice online, it records in QuickBooks instantly. This saves 10+ hours monthly on bookkeeping and eliminates double data entry between systems.

How do I handle chimney repairs on invoices?

Add repair line items directly from your mobile app during the chimney inspection. Common repairs (cap replacement, crown repair, flashing, liner installation) are saved as templates for quick selection. Each line item can have custom pricing, markup from cost, quantity, and detailed description. Photos of damage attach to the invoice for customer transparency and insurance documentation.

Can I customize chimney invoices with my branding?

Absolutely. Upload your chimney business logo, set brand colors, customize payment terms (Net 15, Net 30), add late fees that auto-calculate, include your CSIA certification number, and write custom footer text. You can also create invoice templates for different service types (routine inspection, full cleaning, repair work) to save time.
