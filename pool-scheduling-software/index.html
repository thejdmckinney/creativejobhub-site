Pool Scheduling Software FAQs

How do I set up a recurring weekly pool service schedule?

Add the customer to your system, select "Weekly" as their service frequency, choose which day of the week (e.g., every Monday), and assign to a technician. That's it! The system automatically creates a Monday appointment every week forever. You can pause, skip dates, or adjust the schedule anytime. Takes 2 minutes to set up, then runs automatically.

What if a customer wants to change from weekly to bi-weekly service?

Click on the customer profile, change the service frequency from "Weekly" to "Bi-weekly" (every 2 weeks), and save. The system automatically adjusts all future appointments. Past appointments remain as-is for billing records. Takes 10 seconds to update. You can also pause temporarily for vacations and resume with the same schedule later.

How do customer reminders work?

The system automatically sends reminders 24 hours before each scheduled service. Reminders go via email and/or SMS (customer's choice). Message includes service date, estimated arrival time, technician name, and your company contact info. Customers can respond to reschedule if needed. You can customize the reminder message template with your branding. Unlimited reminders included at $29/mo‚Äîno per-message fees.

Can I see all my technicians' schedules on one calendar?

Yes! The master calendar shows all technicians and their appointments in day, week, or month view. Color-coded by technician or service type. You can filter to show one tech or all. Drag-and-drop appointments to reschedule or reassign to different technicians. Perfect for load balancing and seeing your full operation at a glance. Individual technicians see only their own schedule on mobile.

What happens when pools winterize and need seasonal schedule changes?

You have several options: (1) Pause individual customers' schedules during winter, resume in spring. (2) Bulk-adjust all customers from weekly to bi-weekly winter service. (3) Change service days/times seasonally. (4) Set different billing rates for summer vs winter. The system makes seasonal transitions easy‚Äîselect multiple customers and update schedules in one click. No need to manually reschedule hundreds of appointments.

Does scheduling integrate with route optimization?

Yes! Your scheduled appointments automatically feed into the route optimization system. Each day, the route optimizer looks at all scheduled customers, their locations, and technician assignments, then builds the most efficient route to minimize drive time. Scheduling and routing work together seamlessly. You schedule customers once, routes optimize daily automatically.
