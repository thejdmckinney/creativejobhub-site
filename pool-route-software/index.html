Pool Route Software FAQs

How does route optimization actually work?

Route optimization uses smart algorithms to analyze all your scheduled customers for the day, their locations, estimated service times, and technician availability. It then calculates the most efficient order to visit each stop‚Äîminimizing total drive time. Routes automatically rebuild each night based on the next day's schedule. The system considers traffic patterns, time windows, and customer priorities to create the perfect route.

Can technicians see their route on mobile?

Yes! Technicians open the mobile app and see their complete route for the day in optimized order. Each stop shows customer name, address, service notes, pool details, and chemical history. Tap any customer to get turn-by-turn directions via Google Maps or Apple Maps. The route list updates in real-time if you add/remove stops. Works on any smartphone‚Äîno app download needed.

What if I need to add an emergency service call mid-route?

No problem! Add the emergency customer from the office, and the route automatically re-optimizes to insert them in the best spot. The technician's mobile route updates instantly with the new stop. You can also manually drag-and-drop stops to reorder if you prefer specific sequencing. The system is flexible‚Äîit helps you optimize but doesn't force rigid routing.

Does GPS tracking work all day or just at stops?

GPS tracking works all day. You see the technician's live location on a map‚Äîwhether they're driving between stops or at a customer's property. The system automatically detects when they arrive/depart each stop (using geofencing) and logs the time. This provides proof of service for billing and helps you give customers accurate ETAs if they call asking when the tech will arrive.

Can I assign specific technicians to specific customers?

Absolutely. You can assign customers to specific technicians (e.g., "John always services these 20 pools"). Routes build per technician, so each tech only sees their assigned customers. This is great for maintaining customer relationships and service consistency. You can also have "unassigned" customers that any available technician can service on demand.

What if my technician doesn't have great cell signal on route?

The mobile app works offline! Technicians can view their route, access customer details, log service data, test water chemistry, and take photos‚Äîall without internet. Once they get back in signal range, everything syncs automatically. This is perfect for rural areas or gated communities with poor reception. Your techs never lose productivity due to connectivity issues.
