Plumbing Dispatch Software FAQs

How fast can I dispatch an emergency plumbing call?

With Creative Job Hub, emergency plumbing dispatch takes under 30 seconds. When a customer calls with a burst pipe or water heater failure, you open the dispatch map, see which plumber is closest (with their exact location and current job status), click their name, and hit "Dispatch Emergency." The plumber receives an instant push notification with the customer address, problem type, and priority level. The customer simultaneously receives a text with plumber name, photo, and ETA.

Can dispatch software handle both emergency calls and scheduled jobs?

Yes, Creative Job Hub manages both emergency dispatch and scheduled plumbing jobs simultaneously. Schedule routine maintenance, inspections, and repairs in advance, then when emergencies arise, the system automatically suggests which plumber to dispatch based on proximity, skill level, and schedule flexibility. It can re-route scheduled jobs if needed to accommodate high-priority emergencies.

Does the dispatch software work on mobile?

Yes, both dispatch managers and plumbers have full mobile access. Dispatchers can view the live map, see plumber locations, and dispatch jobs from iPhone or Android. Plumbers receive instant job notifications on their mobile app, view customer details, navigate to the address, and update job status in real-time. Everything syncs instantly between office and field.

What if a plumber's phone battery dies?

The mobile app is optimized for minimal battery drain (5-10% per 8-hour shift with GPS tracking). If a plumber's phone dies or loses signal, the last known location remains visible on the dispatch map with a timestamp. All job data (customer info, service history, photos taken) is backed up to the cloud, so nothing is lost. When the phone powers back on, everything syncs automatically.

How does parts inventory integration help with dispatch?

Before dispatching a job, you can check if the plumber has needed parts on their truck. If they need a specific water heater, pipe size, or specialty part, the system can route them to your warehouse or supplier first, then to the customer. This prevents "I need to go get parts" delays that frustrate customers and waste time. Plumbers can also request parts via the mobile app, and dispatch can coordinate delivery.

Can I customize dispatch rules for my plumbing business?

Yes, Creative Job Hub lets you set custom dispatch rules: prefer specific plumbers for certain customers, automatically assign commercial jobs to master plumbers only, require drain camera techs for specific call types, set maximum drive time limits, and define emergency response zones. The system respects your rules while suggesting the most efficient dispatch options.
