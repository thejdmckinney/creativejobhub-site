Pool Dispatch Software FAQs

What makes pool service dispatch software different from regular dispatch software?

Pool service dispatch software includes features specific to recurring pool maintenance: weekly/bi-weekly route generation, chemical inventory tracking integrated with dispatch, water test logging at each pool, and seasonal schedule adjustments (winter vs. summer service frequencies). Regular dispatch software focuses on one-time service calls, while pool dispatch handles recurring routes more efficiently.

Can I dispatch technicians from my phone?

Yes, Creative Job Hub's mobile app lets you dispatch technicians from anywhere. View the live dispatch map on your phone, see which techs are available, and assign new pool service calls with a few taps. Perfect for owners who want to manage dispatch from the field or while away from the office.

How accurate is the GPS tracking?

GPS tracking is accurate to within 10-30 feet, updated every 30-60 seconds while technicians are on the clock. The system uses geofencing to automatically clock techs in/out when they arrive at/leave pool locations. Battery usage is optimized‚Äîtypically uses 5-10% battery per 8-hour shift with GPS tracking enabled.

What if my tech's phone dies or loses signal?

The mobile app works offline‚Äîtechnicians can view their route, log service visits, record water tests, and take photos without internet. All data syncs automatically when the connection returns. GPS tracking pauses during offline periods but resumes once the signal returns. The last known location remains visible on the dispatch map.

How long does it take to set up dispatch software?

Most pool companies are dispatching within 2-3 hours. Import your customer pool addresses via CSV (or enter manually), add your technicians, and start creating routes. The AI route optimizer generates your first optimized route in under 60 seconds. We offer free onboarding calls to help you set up dispatch workflows, import existing routes, and train your team.

Does dispatch software work for both residential and commercial pools?

Yes, Creative Job Hub handles both residential and commercial pool service. Tag pools by type (residential, HOA, apartment complex, hotel, water park) and set different service frequencies, chemical requirements, and technician assignments.
