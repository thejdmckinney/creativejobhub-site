Regular dispatch software focuses on one-time service calls, while pool dispatch handles recurring routes more efficiently.

Can I dispatch technicians from my phone?

Yes, Creative Job Hub's mobile app lets you dispatch technicians from anywhere. View the live dispatch map on your phone, see which techs are available, and assign new pool service calls with a few taps. Perfect for owners who want to manage dispatch from the field or while away from the office.

How accurate is the GPS tracking?

GPS tracking is accurate to within 10-30 feet, updated every 30-60 seconds while technicians are on the clock.
