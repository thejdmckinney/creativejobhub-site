Frequently Asked Questions About Getting Started

How long does initial setup actually take? Do I need technical skills?

Initial setup takes 30-60 minutes and requires zero technical skills. You'll enter basic business information (name, services, pricing), import existing customer data from a spreadsheet or your old system, and configure your preferences (working hours, service areas, team members). The interface is point-and-click with guided workflows. We provide step-by-step tutorials for each setup task, and many businesses are fully operational within their first day. For businesses transitioning from pen and paper or simple spreadsheets, setup is even faster since you're not migrating from complex systems.

Can I import my existing customer data, or do I have to start from scratch?

You can import all your existing customer data using CSV files (spreadsheets). The system accepts customer information including names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, service history, and custom notes. You can import from Excel, Google Sheets, QuickBooks exports, or other field service software. The import process includes data validation and duplicate detection to ensure clean data. Most businesses complete their customer import in 15-30 minutes. If you're currently using another field service platform, we can help with data migration—contact support and we'll guide you through the export/import process.

How do I get my technicians trained on the mobile app?

Technician training takes 15-30 minutes per person. The mobile app is designed to be intuitive—if your techs can use a smartphone, they can use Creative Job Hub. We provide short video tutorials (3-5 minutes each) covering: viewing job details, navigating to job sites, completing jobs with photos and signatures, and creating invoices. Many businesses do a 20-minute training session showing the core workflow: check today's schedule, start a job, complete it, get a signature, convert to invoice. Techs typically become comfortable within their first 2-3 jobs. The app works offline, so techs can complete jobs even in areas with poor cell coverage.

Do I need to set up everything in Week 1, or can I phase features in gradually?

The 4-week timeline is a suggested best practice, not a requirement. Many businesses start with just scheduling and invoicing in Week 1, then add features gradually as they're comfortable. You can use Creative Job Hub as a simple scheduling tool initially and activate automations, marketing campaigns, and advanced features later. The system works at whatever pace fits your business. Some businesses go all-in and activate everything in the first week, while others spend 2-3 months exploring features. The key is starting with core workflows (scheduling, job completion, invoicing) and expanding from there. All features remain available indefinitely at the same $89/month price.

What if I get stuck or need help during setup?

Help is available 24/7 through multiple channels. The in-app live chat connects you with support instantly during business hours (responses within 5-10 minutes), and after hours you'll get a response within 2-4 hours. We also offer video tutorials for every major feature, a comprehensive help center with step-by-step guides, email support at support@creativejobhub.com, and scheduled onboarding calls for personalized walkthroughs. Many setup questions are answered through contextual help tips built into the interface. If you're stuck on data import, scheduling configuration, or any setup task, support can screen-share and guide you through it. We're invested in your success—you're not left to figure things out alone.

Can I try the system before committing to a paid plan?

Yes, Creative Job Hub offers a free trial with full access to all features—no credit card required to start. You can explore the entire platform, import your data, schedule jobs, use the mobile app, and test all features without any payment commitment. The trial period gives you enough time to evaluate whether Creative Job Hub fits your business needs. During the trial, you have full access to support and onboarding resources. Many businesses complete the entire 4-week getting started process during their trial period. If you decide Creative Job Hub isn't right for you, simply let the trial expire with no charges. If you love it, subscribe for $89/month to continue with all your data intact.
