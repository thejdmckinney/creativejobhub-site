FAQ

Pricing & billing questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes. Starter $300/yr, Team $468/yr, Pro $948/yr. That's effectively $25, $39, and $79 per month when billed annually.

What's included in every plan?

Scheduling, estimates, invoices, payments, client alerts, time tracking, Mapbox live tracking, QuickBooks Online, Google Drive, Twilio and Resend integrations, and a mobile-optimized experience.

Are there hidden fees or add-ons?

No. We don't nickel-and-dime. All core features are included. Bring your own Stripe/Twilio/Resend accounts for transaction or messaging fees.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Try any plan free for 7 days. No credit card required to start. Full access to all features during your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor.
