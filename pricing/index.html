FAQ

Pricing & billing questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Lite is free forever for a single seat with usage limits. Upgrade when you’re ready.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yep—annual plans are discounted vs. paying month-to-month. You’ll see the savings when you toggle “Pay annually.”

What’s included in paid plans?

Everything in Lite, plus advanced scheduling, SMS, QuickBooks Online sync, permissions, analytics with AI insights, and more (see plan details above).

How do cancellations and refunds work?

Cancel anytime from your account settings. We’ll stop future renewals. If something goes sideways, contact support and we’ll help.

Do you charge per technician or per seat?

Plans include a set number of seats. Add more seats as needed; pricing updates instantly.
