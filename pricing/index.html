FAQ

Pricing & billing questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Starter $300/yr, Team $468/yr, Pro $948/yr. That’s effectively $25, $39, and $79 per month when billed annually.

What’s included in every plan?

Scheduling, estimates, invoices, payments, client alerts, Mapbox live tracking, time tracking, QuickBooks Online & Google Drive integrations, and a mobile-optimized experience.

Are there hidden fees or add-ons?

No. Bring your own Stripe/Twilio/Resend accounts for transaction or messaging costs; we don’t add markups.
