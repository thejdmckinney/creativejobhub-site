Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Creative Job Hub:

  • Account & profile — name, email, phone, company, role and other profile fields.
  • Location — precise (GPS) technician location for live tracking, routing and dispatch.
  • Content you upload — photos, notes, documents and attachments related to jobs.
  • Payments & billing metadata — subscription and invoice metadata (payments processed by Stripe).
  • Device, logs & usage — device identifiers, OS version, crash logs and analytics data.
  • Communications — messages, SMS and emails used for service and support.

2. How we use information

We use personal data to:

  • Provide and operate service features — scheduling, dispatch, jobs, and maps;
  • Enable live tracking and routing for technicians;
  • Process invoices and payments via Stripe;
  • Send job and account notifications, and provide support;
  • Analyze usage and improve the product; and
  • Protect against fraud and secure the platform.

3. Location collection and use

Precise location is collected only with explicit permission. Location is used for live technician tracking, route optimization, and arrival estimates. Typically:

  • Location is collected while a technician is on an active job or explicitly enables tracking;
  • Users can stop location collection by disabling app location permission in device settings or by toggling tracking off in-app;
  • Location is retained for troubleshooting and dispute resolution — see retention below.
Suggested retention: precise location data is retained up to 30 days after a job completes, unless you request earlier deletion.

4. Third parties and processors

We use third parties to operate the Service. Key processors (and links to their privacy pages):

  • Supabase — backend & storage
  • Mapbox — maps & geolocation
  • Stripe — payments
  • Twilio — SMS & communications
  • Sentry / Firebase — optional crash/analytics (if enabled)

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with law enforcement or to comply with legal process when required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as needed. Typical retention windows:

Data categoryRetention
Account & profileUntil account deletion + 1 year
Job data, photos, documentsUntil job deletion or up to 2 years after completion
Location dataUp to 30 days after job completion
Payments & invoices7 years (tax/accounting compliance)
Logs & analyticsUp to 180 days (aggregated/anonymized where possible)

6. Security

We use industry standard protections (TLS in transit, access controls) to protect data. No method is 100% secure, but we apply reasonable safeguards and limit access to authorized personnel only.

7. International transfers

Data may be transferred to servers outside your country (for example the United States). We apply contractual safeguards where required by law.

8. Your choices & rights

You can request access, correction, restriction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing hello@creativejobhub.com. We will verify requests and respond according to applicable law. To stop location tracking, disable the app's location permission in your device settings or toggle tracking off in the app.

9. Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers. For disputes or refunds contact support at hello@creativejobhub.com.

10. Children

This app is for professional use and is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable local age). If you believe a child has provided data without consent, contact us to remove the data.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with an updated “Last updated” date.
