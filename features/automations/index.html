Campaign Automations

Scheduled and recurring email campaigns that run on autopilot

Scheduled Campaigns

Set it and forget it email marketing:

  • One-time campaigns (send on specific date/time)
  • Recurring campaigns (weekly or monthly)
  • Segmented targeting (specific customer groups)
  • Personalized merge fields ({{client_name}})
  • Performance metrics tracked automatically
  • Real-time campaign analytics

Campaign Types

Perfect for every marketing need:

  • Seasonal promotions ("Fall furnace tune-up")
  • Maintenance reminders ("Annual inspection due")
  • Loyalty rewards ("Thank you for 5 years")
  • Service updates ("New services available")
  • Educational content ("5 signs you need repair")
  • Special offers and discounts

Smart Segmentation

Target the right customers:

  • All clients (broadcast to everyone)
  • Custom segments (filter by criteria)
  • Service history based (HVAC customers only)
  • Location based (specific service areas)
  • Purchase history (high-value customers)
  • Custom lists (CSV import)

Merge Fields

Personalize every email automatically:

  • {{client_name}} - Personalized greeting
  • {{company_name}} - Your business name
  • {{last_job_date}} - Last service date
  • {{booking_link}} - Direct booking link
  • {{portal_link}} - Customer portal access
  • {{unsubscribe_link}} - Compliance link

Campaign Analytics

Track performance in real-time:

  • Total emails sent
  • Open rates percentage
  • Click-through rates
  • Conversion tracking (bookings)
  • Revenue generated per campaign
  • Unsubscribe rates

Campaign Automation Process

Simple 7-step workflow:

  • 1. Create campaign in Marketing page
  • 2. Choose audience (segment or all)
  • 3. Write email content with merge fields
  • 4. Schedule date/time or recurring
  • 5. System sends automatically
  • 6. Performance tracked in real-time
  • 7. Results visible in Marketing Analytics
