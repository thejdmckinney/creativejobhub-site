Job Status Visualization

Pie chart showing distribution of jobs by status (Scheduled, In Progress, Complete, On Hold, Cancelled). Color-coded with percentages and counts. High "In Progress" indicates bottlenecks, high "On Hold" suggests operational issues, high "Cancelled" signals customer satisfaction problems.

Revenue & Expense Trends

12-month line chart showing three trends: Revenue (green), Expenses (red), and Profit (blue). Hover for exact values. Identifies seasonal patterns, tracks profit margin trends, spots unusual spikes, monitors growth, and helps plan for slow seasons.

Top Clients by Revenue

Horizontal bar chart showing your top 10 clients by all-time revenue. Identifies VIP clients for retention focus, guides loyalty program design, reveals upsell opportunities, supports risk management, and helps profile ideal customers for sales targeting.

Customer Segmentation (RFM Model)

Know Your Customer Types: Advanced segmentation algorithm uses Recency (days since last service), Frequency (number of jobs), and Monetary value (total revenue) to categorize customers into 5 actionable segments. Includes pie chart (percentage distribution), bar chart (revenue by segment), and detailed table with metrics.

Champions (10-15%)

Profile: Spend >$5K, 5+ jobs, active <30 days
Action: Protect at all costs—VIP treatment, loyalty programs, exclusive perks, priority scheduling, personal account manager

Loyal Customers (20-30%)

Profile: Spend >$2K, 3+ jobs, active <90 days
Action: Stable revenue base—maintain quality, ask for referrals, upsell additional services, quarterly check-ins

At Risk (15-20%)

Profile: Spent >$3K, but inactive 90-180 days
Action: Urgent re-engagement—special offers, personal phone calls, "we miss you" campaigns, service reminders

New Customers (20-25%)

Profile: First service <90 days, 1-2 jobs
Action: Nurture relationship—follow-up emails, thank-you notes, ask for feedback, introduce service packages, build trust

Lost (20-30%)

Profile: Inactive >180 days, no recent bookings
Action: Win-back campaigns—aggressive discounts, seasonal reminders, "special offer just for you," survey to understand why they left

Predictive Analytics & Maintenance Recommendations

AI Predicts Customer Needs: Machine learning analyzes customer service history to identify patterns (annual HVAC tune-ups, seasonal work, equipment lifecycle) and predicts when customers will need service next. Includes confidence scores, predicted dates, and one-click scheduling. Proactive outreach increases retention by 20-30%.

Proactive Scheduling

AI predicts service dates and recommends calling customers before they call you. "Mrs. Johnson is due for annual furnace tune-up in 3 weeks (85% confidence). Call now to book." Increases bookings by 15-20%.

Preventive Maintenance

Automated reminders for recurring services based on equipment type, age, and usage patterns. "HVAC systems need tune-up every 12 months. Last service: 10 months ago. Schedule now." Reduces emergency calls by 30%.

Churn Risk Scoring

AI calculates likelihood customer will leave (0-100% risk score) based on service frequency, recency, satisfaction, and payment history. "Client at 78% churn risk. Last service 145 days ago. Reach out immediately." Saves 20-30% of at-risk customers.

Upsell Opportunities

AI identifies services customers are likely to need based on current services, property type, and seasonal factors. "Customer has HVAC maintenance but no plumbing plan. 72% likely to buy if offered." Increases upsell conversion by 25%.

Lifetime Value Projection

Predicts future revenue from each customer based on service frequency, average ticket size, and retention probability. "Projected LTV: $8,500 over 5 years. Champion segment. Prioritize retention." Guides resource allocation.

Optimal Contact Timing

AI learns when customers are most responsive (time of day, day of week, seasonality) and recommends best time to reach out. "Best contact window: Weekday mornings 9-11am (42% response rate)." Improves engagement by 30%.
