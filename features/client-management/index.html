Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about client management

What's included in the 360-degree customer view?

Every customer profile shows: complete job history with dates and services, all communication (calls, emails, texts, notes), financial history (total spent, outstanding balances, payment methods), property details and service locations, equipment and service history, preferences and special instructions, loyalty points and referral activity, and churn risk indicators. Everything your team needs to provide personalized service is in one place.

How does churn prediction work?

The system analyzes customer behavior patterns: time since last service, declining job frequency, reduced spending, missed appointments, and lack of engagement. When multiple warning signs appear, the customer is flagged as "at risk" with a churn probability score. You get proactive alerts so you can reach out before they leave. Early intervention with personalized win-back offers typically saves 40-60% of at-risk customers.

Can I import my existing customer data?

Yes! Import customers from CSV files, spreadsheets, or directly from QuickBooks Online. The system maps fields automatically and validates data during import. You can import contact info, service history, equipment details, and financial data. For migrations from other field service software (Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan), we provide free migration assistance to ensure nothing is lost in the transition.

How does customer segmentation help my business?

Segmentation lets you target the right customers with the right message. Create segments like: high-value customers (for VIP treatment), customers who haven't booked in 6+ months (for win-back campaigns), customers with specific equipment (for maintenance reminders), customers in certain zip codes (for area promotions). Each segment can have automated campaigns and customized messaging, dramatically improving your marketing effectiveness and ROI.

Does the CRM integrate with QuickBooks?

Yes! Full two-way sync with QuickBooks Online. Customer data, invoices, payments, and balances automatically sync between systems. When you create or update a customer in Creative Job Hub, it syncs to QuickBooks and vice versa. Financial data in your CRM is always current without manual entry. This eliminates duplicate work and ensures your books are always accurate.
