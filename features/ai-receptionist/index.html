Real Call Scenarios

See how AI Receptionist handles common customer interactions

Emergency Service

Customer: "I have a burst pipe flooding my basement!"

AI: "I understand this is an emergency. Let me collect your information quickly. What's your name and phone number?"

[Customer provides info]

AI: "I've created an emergency service request for you, and our on-call technician will contact you within 15 minutes. In the meantime, if you know where your main water shutoff is, turning that off can help stop the flooding. Is there anything else I can help with right now?"

What Happens:
  • Lead created with "emergency" priority
  • Notification sent to on-call technician
  • Immediate follow-up triggered within 15 minutes
  • Customer feels heard and helped

Pricing Question

Customer: "How much do you charge for a furnace tune-up?"

AI: "Our standard furnace tune-up typically starts at $150. This includes a complete inspection, filter change, and system testing. However, if any repairs or parts are needed, that would be additional. I can take your information and have our office provide you with a detailed quote. What's your name and phone number?"

What Happens:
  • AI quotes from your price book
  • Clarifies it's a starting price
  • Collects customer info for accurate quote
  • Creates lead for follow-up

Scheduling Request

Customer: "I need my drains cleaned. Can you come out Tuesday morning?"

AI: "I'd be happy to help you schedule a drain cleaning. Let me collect some information. What's your name and phone number? And what's the full address where we'll be working?"

[Customer provides details]

AI: "Great! I've noted your preference for Tuesday morning, and I've created a service request for drain cleaning at 123 Main Street. Our office will call you within 2 hours to confirm the exact appointment time. Is there anything else I can help with?"

What Happens:
  • Lead created with scheduling preference
  • Office staff confirms specific time (prevents conflicts)
  • No over-promising or double-booking
  • Customer satisfied with fast response

General Inquiry

Customer: "What services do you offer?"

AI: "We offer a full range of plumbing services including drain cleaning, leak repair, water heater installation and repair, toilet repair, faucet repair, and emergency plumbing services. We also do remodeling plumbing for kitchens and bathrooms. Is there a specific service you're interested in?"

Customer: "Yeah, I think I have a leak under my kitchen sink."

AI: "I can definitely help with that. Let me get your information so we can schedule someone to take a look. What's your name and phone number?"

What Happens:
  • AI provides complete service list
  • Asks clarifying questions
  • Continues conversation naturally
  • Creates lead when customer wants service
