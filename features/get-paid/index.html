Frequently Asked Questions About Getting Paid with Stripe

What are the actual transaction fees for accepting online payments through Stripe?

Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30 per successful card transaction, which is the standard rate for credit and debit cards. ACH bank transfers cost 0.8% with a $5 maximum per transaction. Apple Pay and Google Pay use the same 2.9% + $0.30 rate as cards. Creative Job Hub does NOT add any platform fees or markups—you pay exactly what you'd pay using Stripe directly. This is significantly better than many competitors who charge 3.5-5% or add platform fees on top of processing fees. Your $89/month subscription includes unlimited payment processing with no additional Creative Job Hub fees.

How does automatic reconciliation work? Do I need to manually match payments?

Automatic reconciliation is 100% hands-off. When a customer pays online through Stripe, the system receives instant webhook notifications and automatically marks the invoice as "Paid," updates the customer's balance, logs the payment with date and amount, and sends a receipt. This happens in 2-3 seconds with zero manual work. You never need to log into Stripe to match payments or update invoices. The system handles everything automatically, even for partial payments. Manual reconciliation (matching bank deposits to invoices) typically takes 2-4 hours per week—automatic reconciliation eliminates this entirely.

When does money from Stripe payments actually hit my bank account?

Stripe transfers funds to your bank account on a rolling basis—typically 2 business days after a payment is received for established accounts. For example, payments received on Monday arrive in your bank on Wednesday. New Stripe accounts may have a 7-day initial hold for the first transfers as a standard fraud prevention measure, but this shortens to 2 days after a few weeks of processing history. You can configure your payout schedule in Stripe (daily, weekly, or monthly). All Stripe payouts are automatic—you don't need to initiate transfers. You can track pending and completed payouts in both Stripe and Creative Job Hub dashboards.

Is it secure? How is customer payment information protected?

Stripe is PCI Level 1 certified, the highest security standard in the payment industry—the same security level used by major banks and enterprises. Customer payment information never touches Creative Job Hub servers. When customers pay, they enter card details directly on Stripe's secure checkout page (Stripe Checkout), and all data is encrypted end-to-end. Creative Job Hub only receives a secure token confirming the payment was successful. This means you have zero liability for data breaches. Stripe handles all security updates, fraud detection, and compliance requirements automatically. Your customers' payment data is as secure as when they shop on Amazon or pay through any major retailer.

Can customers save their payment method for faster checkout on future invoices?

Yes, Stripe automatically saves payment methods for returning customers (with their permission). When a customer pays their first invoice, Stripe creates a customer record and securely saves their card on file. On subsequent invoices, customers can either use their saved card with one click or enter a new payment method. This dramatically improves convenience—returning customers can pay in 10-15 seconds instead of re-entering card details. Saved payment methods are stored securely in Stripe's vault and are never visible in Creative Job Hub. Customers can manage their saved cards through the customer portal, and you can charge saved payment methods for recurring services or subscriptions with customer authorization.

What happens if a customer disputes a charge or requests a refund?

For refunds, you can process full or partial refunds directly from Creative Job Hub with one click. The refund processes through Stripe (typically 5-10 business days to customer's card), and the system automatically updates the invoice status and customer balance. For disputes (chargebacks), Stripe notifies you immediately via email and in the dashboard. You have the opportunity to provide evidence (photos, signed documents, communication logs) to contest the dispute. Stripe handles the dispute process with the card issuer. If you lose a dispute, Stripe deducts the disputed amount plus a $15 chargeback fee. Dispute rates in field services average 0.1-0.3%—very rare when you provide quality service and documentation. Creative Job Hub's photo documentation and digital signatures significantly help win disputes.
