Invoice Status Workflow

Draft

Invoice created but not sent. Can be edited freely. Not visible to customer.

Sent

Invoice emailed to customer with PDF and payment link. Counted in Outstanding.

Partial

Some payment received. Balance still due. Shows amount paid and remaining.

Paid

Full payment received. Balance due = $0. Marked complete and archived.

Overdue

Past due date with balance outstanding. Automatic status change triggers alerts.
