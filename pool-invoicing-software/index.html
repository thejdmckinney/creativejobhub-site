How do I set up recurring monthly invoices for pool customers?

Add the customer to your system with their billing frequency (monthly, bi-weekly, weekly). Set the billing day (e.g., 1st of every month). That's it! Every month on the 1st, the system automatically generates an invoice with all services performed that month, emails it to the customer with a 'Pay Now' button. You never manually create invoices. Takes 2 minutes to set up per customer, then runs forever.

Do I need a separate merchant account for online payments?
