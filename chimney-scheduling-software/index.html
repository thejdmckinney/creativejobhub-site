Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up annual recurring chimney appointments?

Set up annual recurring appointments once per customer, and the system automatically generates their next year's appointment. Most chimney sweeps schedule customers for September-November (fall rush), and the software handles all the scheduling automatically. You can set different frequencies (annual, semi-annual) and skip years if a customer moves or sells their home.

Can I change a customer's chimney service frequency?

Yes, you can easily adjust any customer's recurring schedule. For example, if a customer with heavy wood burning needs bi-annual cleaning instead of annual, just update their frequency and the system adjusts future appointments automatically. This is common for customers who burn wood all winter and need more frequent creosote removal.

How do automated customer reminders work?

The software automatically sends email and SMS reminders to customers before their chimney appointment (typically 24-48 hours ahead). This reduces no-shows by 60% during busy fall season. Customers can confirm, reschedule, or ask questions directly from the reminder message. All reminders are unlimited and included at no extra cost.

What is the master chimney calendar and how does it work?

The master calendar shows all your technicians' chimney appointments in day, week, or month views. You can see your entire fall rush season at a glance, drag-and-drop to reschedule appointments, and balance workload across multiple chimney sweeps. Color coding shows appointment types (inspection, cleaning, repair), and you can sync with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook.

How do I manage seasonal scheduling for chimney services?

Chimney businesses have heavy fall/winter demand and slow spring/summer periods. The scheduling software lets you bulk-schedule all annual customers for September-November in one click, set seasonal pricing (winter premium rates), and pause scheduling during slow months. You can also set capacity limits to avoid overbooking during peak season.

Does chimney scheduling integrate with route optimization?

Yes, appointments automatically feed into daily route optimization. When you schedule a chimney appointment, the system considers the home's location and groups it with other nearby jobs on the same day. This means your technicians get efficient routes without any extra work, saving 2+ hours daily in drive time during busy fall season.
