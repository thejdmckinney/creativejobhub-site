Pool Billing Software FAQs

Can I bill different amounts for different pools?

Yes, every customer can have a custom monthly rate. Set different pricing for weekly vs bi-weekly service, residential vs commercial pools, large vs small pools, or premium vs basic service packages. You can also add one-time charges for repairs, extra chemicals, equipment, or special treatments that appear on that month's invoice only.

What if a customer wants to switch from monthly to bi-weekly billing?

Changing billing frequency takes 10 seconds‚Äîjust edit the customer profile and select the new billing cycle. The system automatically adjusts their next invoice date and amount. If they're mid-cycle, you can pro-rate the current period or wait until the next billing cycle. You can also pause billing temporarily (for vacations or winterization) and resume when service restarts.

How do online payments work?

Customers click the "Pay Now" button on their invoice email or customer portal. They enter credit card or bank account info on a secure payment page. Funds are processed immediately and deposited to your bank account in 1-2 business days. Payment processing fees are 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction for credit cards, or 1% (max $10) for ACH bank transfers. All payments automatically sync to QuickBooks.

Can customers set up automatic payments?

Yes! Customers can enable auto-pay in their portal. Once enabled, their saved payment method is automatically charged when each invoice is generated. They still receive the invoice for their records, but payment happens automatically. This dramatically reduces late payments and improves your cash flow. Customers can disable auto-pay anytime.

What if a customer's payment fails?

If a payment fails (expired card, insufficient funds), the customer receives an email notification asking them to update their payment method. You're also notified so you can follow up if needed. The system can automatically retry the payment after a specified number of days. You can also manually charge a different payment method or accept offline payments (cash, check) which you record in the system.

Does pool billing software work for seasonal businesses?

Yes, Creative Job Hub is perfect for seasonal pool businesses. Pause recurring billing when pools are winterized/closed, resume in spring. Set up different seasonal pricing (winter maintenance rates vs summer full-service rates). You can also bill annually in advance, or use graduated pricing (e.g., $120/mo May-Sept, $60/mo Oct-Apr). The system handles all seasonal billing scenarios.
