Frequently Asked Questions

How does chimney route optimization work?

Our AI-powered chimney route optimization software automatically arranges your daily chimney sweep appointments in the most efficient order based on geographic proximity, appointment duration, and traffic patterns. The system considers seasonal factors like fall rush periods, CSIA inspection requirements, and travel time between homes. This typically saves 2+ hours per day and reduces fuel costs by 20-30% during busy seasons.

Can I view my chimney routes on mobile?

Yes, our mobile app provides turn-by-turn navigation for your optimized chimney routes. Technicians can see their daily schedule organized by location, with one-tap navigation to each home. The app works offline during inspections and syncs when back online, perfect for basement chimney work where cell service may be limited.

How do I handle emergency chimney calls with route optimization?

Emergency chimney services (like urgent creosote buildup or chimney fires) can be instantly added to any technician's route. The software automatically re-optimizes the remaining stops to accommodate the emergency call while minimizing impact to scheduled appointments. You can also send automated notifications to affected customers about any delays.

Does the GPS tracking work for chimney inspections?

Yes, GPS tracking captures exact arrival and departure times at each chimney location, even when technicians are working inside homes or on rooftops. Customers receive automatic notifications when your chimney sweep is on the way and when service is complete. This provides proof of service for CSIA compliance documentation and insurance claims.

Can I assign specific chimneys to specific technicians?

Absolutely. You can assign specific CSIA-certified technicians to customers who prefer consistent service, or let the system auto-assign based on geographic location, certification level (Level 1/2/3 inspections), and seasonal workload. This ensures your most experienced sweeps handle complex jobs while newer technicians focus on routine cleanings.

Does chimney route software work offline?

Yes, our mobile app downloads your daily chimney routes and works completely offline. Technicians can navigate to homes, complete CSIA inspection checklists, log creosote levels, take photos, and collect payments without internet. Everything syncs automatically when back online, perfect for rural areas or when working inside homes with poor cell reception.
